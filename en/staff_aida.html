Masaki Aida Laboratory

Professor Masaki Aida

Information Network / Network Science / Autonomous Distributed Control / Social Media Network / Social Network Analysis

Due to the development of information networks, phenomena on the Internet become to have a great influence on actual social activities in the real world. In particular, explosive user dynamics such as flaming can cause widespread social losses. The latest theme in our laboratory is to understand the cause of the occurrence of the explosive user dynamics from an engineering viewpoint and to realize some suppression technologies of the occurrence. In this process, by pursuing the causality of user dynamics, we are led to a world where information network engineering and relativistic quantum theory are naturally fused.
